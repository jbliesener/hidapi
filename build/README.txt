*** Create your target-specific build directories here. Then, in each of them,  ***
*** run 'cmake -DCMAKE_TOOLCHAIN_FILE=<toolchainFile> ../..' and 'make'.        ***
***                                                                             ***
*** For example:                                                                ***
***     cd build                                                                ***
***     mkdir win32                                                             ***
***     cmake -DCMAKE_TOOLCHAIN_FILE=../../cmake/i686-w64-mingw32.cmake ../..   ***
***     make                                                                    ***
***                                                                             ***
*** This creates platform-specific directories under 'libs' and 'progs', where  ***
*** the compiled programs are stored.                                           ***
